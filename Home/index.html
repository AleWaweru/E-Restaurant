ABOUT US

Who We Are

Driven with the love for good food, talking of mouth watering meals,Tamu Dishes came into play. Inspired by the desire to try new dishes, and share with the rest of the world, Tamu Dishes was established in year 2023 as an online food order restaurant. It was established with the main purpose being serving the busy population of Nairobi CBD with quality meals, at a pocket friendly price, making in person deliveries at their place of preference.InThe near future, the company aims at open more remote branchs in Kenya and beyond.
